# T-Mobile Reseller Partnership — Action Plan

## Overview

There are **3 paths** to resell T-Mobile connectivity:

| Path | Best For | Requirements | Time to Launch |
|------|----------|--------------|----------------|
| **1. Partner Program** | Solution providers bundling connectivity | Business entity, sales capability | 2-4 weeks |
| **2. Primary Agent (Master Dealer)** | High-volume resellers | Through distributor (BMP, GetWireless) | 1-2 weeks |
| **3. MVNO/Wholesale** | Building your own ISP brand | Significant volume commitment | 3-6 months |

---

## Recommended Path: Partner Program + Primary Agent

For Origin OS retail stack, this hybrid approach works best:

**Portal:** https://businesspartners.t-mobile.com

- Access to T-Mobile 5G Business Internet

- Valid business entity (LLC/Corp)

**T-Mobile 5G Business Internet:**

## Commission Structure (Reference)

From T-Mobile partner documentation:

**Standard Commissions:**
- New line activation: $X per line (varies by plan)
- Business Internet: Higher payout than voice lines
- Hardware/MDM: Additional compensation available
- SPIFF bonuses: Limited-time additional payouts

**Value Added Solution (VAS) Opportunities:**
- Submit Statement of Work (SOW) for custom solutions
- Higher compensation for bundled solutions
- Requires T-Mobile approval
